How to Choose an AI Writing Tool

Consider these factors:

  • Budget: Free plans (e.g., Copy.ai) vs. paid (e.g., Jasper).
  • Use Case: Marketing (Jasper) vs. general writing (Grammarly).
  • Ease of Use: User-friendly (Copy.ai) vs. feature-rich (Jasper).

X User (@WriterBot): “Picked Copy.ai for its free plan—perfect for my blog!” (Source: X post, 2025).

How to Use Canva’s AI for Free

Steps to leverage Canva’s Magic Studio:

  1. Sign up for a free Canva account.
  2. Use Magic Design for text-to-image creation.
  3. Explore templates for social media posts.
